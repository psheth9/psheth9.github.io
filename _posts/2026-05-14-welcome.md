This is the first post on the new site. Quick note on what to expect here, then we'll get to the actual writing.

## What I'll be writing about

Three topics, mostly:

1. **Ads infrastructure & ML systems** — what I work on day-to-day at Meta. Things like ranking, delivery control, feature stores, and the surprisingly hard problem of *not* showing the same ad too many times.
2. **LLM tooling for engineers** — I've been building an internal Ads-debug agent using RAG + tool use. Lots to share about what works and what doesn't.
3. **War stories from FinTech & crypto** — I spent years at Barclays, Robinhood, and Stellar before Meta. There are some good ones.

## What I won't be writing about

- Hot takes on tech twitter
- "How I 10x'd my productivity" lifehacks
- Anything that requires you to subscribe to a newsletter to finish reading

## How this is built

This site is static — Jekyll, GitHub Pages, no analytics, no tracking. The source is open if you want to copy any of it.

Notes (algorithms + system design) live at [`/notes`](/notes). Resume + recommendations on [the homepage](/).

More soon.
